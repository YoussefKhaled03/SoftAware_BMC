SoftAware

Strategic Business Model Canvas | Validated Dec 2025

Key Partners

    Talent Pipeline (Supply)

  • ITI & NTI: Primary source for vetted Junior/Mid talent.
  • DEPI & DECI: Digital Egypt initiatives for fresh graduates.

    • Infrastructure & Payment

  • Cloud Providers: AWS / Azure (For hosting client projects).
  • Fawry / Paymob: Local payment gateways to build trust with Egyptian SMEs.

    • Client Acquisition (Demand)

  • ITIDA & Creativa Hubs: Source for funded startups needing technical execution.
  • Incubators (TIEC): Funneling non-technical founders to us.

Key Activities

  • Vetting & QA: Code reviews (Tier 2) and Architecture (Tier 1).
  • Automated Matchmaking: Algorithm assigning the right team to the request.
  • Sales & B2B Partnerships: Closing deals with SMEs/Startups.
  • Lifecycle Management: Monitoring client growth to upsell features (e.g., scaling servers).

Key Resources

  • The Platform: Our proprietary task assignment system.
  • The Talent Pool: Database of 500+ vetted engineers.
  • Client Intelligence Engine: Data on client tech stacks used to suggest upgrades (e.g., "You have a web app, do you need a mobile app?").
  • Operational Team: Support staff and account managers bridging clients and freelancers.
  • Vetting Data: Historic performance data of freelancers.

Value Propositions

    For Clients

  • Guaranteed Delivery: "Agency Quality at Freelance Prices."
  • Zero Admin Headache: We handle the team; you get the product.
  • Trust & Local Payments: Pay via Fawry/Local Bank, deal with a legal entity.

    • For Freelancers

  • Income Security: Guaranteed payment (no ghosting).
  • Mentorship: Juniors get reviewed by Seniors.
  • Focus on Code: No need to do sales or bidding wars.

Customer Relationships

  • Automated Dashboard: For standard requests and tracking.
  • Dedicated Account Manager: For VIP/Enterprise clients (High touch).
  • Meritocratic System: Freelancers advance tiers based on code quality, not just "likes."

Channels

  • Direct Sales: LinkedIn outreach to Founders.
  • Partnership Ecosystem: ITIDA/Creativa events.
  • Referral Program: Discounts for startups referring other founders.
  • University Hubs: For recruiting talent.

Customer Segments

    Primary (Demand)

  • Non-Tech Founders: Have funding/ideas but no tech team.
  • SMEs: Traditional businesses digitizing operations.
  • Agencies: Outsourcing overflow work.

    • Secondary (Supply)

  • Software Engineers: Juniors (Execution), Mids (Review), Seniors (Architecture).

Cost Structure

  • Core Team & Ops Salaries: Support team, Sales, and Matchmaking managers.
  • Marketing & CAC: Ads, event sponsorship, and referral costs.
  • Tech Infrastructure: Server hosting, domain, and platform maintenance.
  • Legal & Admin: Licensing and payment gateway fees.

Revenue Streams

  • Project Margin: 40% Net Revenue on every development project.
  • Managed Hosting (MRR): Reselling cloud hosting (AWS/Azure) with a management fee.
  • Deployment Services: One-time fees for setting up client infrastructure.
  • Priority Support: Retainers for post-launch maintenance.
